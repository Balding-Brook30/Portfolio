About Me

I got into web development because I liked the idea of building things that actually help people. Not just “websites for fun,” but tools that help someone grow their business or get more clients.

Right now I’m focused on working with local businesses—especially barbers—because I see how much talent exists without the right online presence to match it. My goal is to bridge that gap with simple, effective websites that actually do their job.

I keep things straightforward. No confusing process. No unnecessary steps. Just clean design and results.
